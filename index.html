WHAT WE DO
Dominca solves problems related to impacts, shock waves, and high strain-rate phenomena with the best analytical, computational, and experimental techniques.

We frequently tackle materials that are non-linear and inhomogeneous, as well as strain-rate-sensitive (frequency) and temperature-sensitive. Such materials present complex engineering challenges, requiring advanced theory and intricate simulation.
